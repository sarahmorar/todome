# Todome — Do It Smart

A smart to-do app that automatically prioritizes your tasks so you can focus on what actually matters.

---

## What it does

Todome takes your tasks and instantly assigns a priority based on urgency, deadlines, and context.
Instead of manually organizing everything, you just write:

“Finish project report tomorrow at 5pm”

and Todome figures out the rest.

## Why Todome?

Most to-do apps make you manage priorities manually. Most of us don't have time for that.

Todome removes that friction:
- Less organizing
- More doing
- Better focus

---

## Key Features

- **Automatic prioritization**
  - Tasks are ranked from Low → Overdue
  - Adjusts as time passes

- **Real-time updates**
  - A task due tomorrow becomes more urgent automatically

- **Natural task input**
  - Just type your task normally — no special formatting needed

- **Smart filtering**
  - Focus on High / Critical / Overdue tasks instantly

- **Categories**
  - Organize tasks with custom labels

- **Desktop app**
  - Runs locally on your computer
  - No internet required
